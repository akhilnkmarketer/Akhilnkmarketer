SEO

is the practice of optimizing a website to improve its ranking and visibility on search engine results pages (SERPs), particularly for search engines like Google, Bing, or Yahoo. The goal of SEO is to drive organic traffic to a website by making it more relevant and accessible to users and search engines.

SMM

is the use of social media platforms to promote products, services, or brands, engage with audiences, and build brand awareness. It involves creating and sharing content tailored to the specific social media channels being used, as well as paid advertising campaigns to reach a broader audience.

SEM

is a digital marketing strategy that involves promoting websites by increasing their visibility in search engine results pages (SERPs) through paid advertising. It primarily refers to pay-per-click (PPC) advertising, where businesses bid on keywords to display their ads on search engines like Google and Bing.
